Camp Crystal Lake Design is a design layout made as a poster or image to look at using HTML and CSS. The design is made using flex grids. The banner in the top left is a banner of serial killer Jason Voorhees in a grave yard. There is a timeline of Crystal Lake in the right column. The bottom left is a flag of Camp Crystal. The bottom right is a image of Friday the 13th and a dvd with the price on Amazon.
